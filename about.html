About

Hi - I’m Patricia.

I’ve spent most of my career working in climate - and the last 8+ years specifically focused on carbon removal - not just behind spreadsheets, but also close to projects, developers, and buyers shaping this market.

I’ve advised hundreds of carbon removal developers and worked with organisations purchasing removals, helping them navigate topics like MRV, durability, contracting, pricing, and climate claims - always with a focus on integrity and long-term value.

In 2024, I travelled extensively across the Global South, visiting projects and working on the ground with developers - an experience that reinforced how I see climate solutions: as something built through execution, not narratives.

Alongside advisory work, I’m a Venture Partner at Satgana, investing in early-stage climate solutions.

If you’re working on carbon removal, carbon markets, or climate strategy - and looking for pragmatic, integrity-first support - I’d love to connect.
