About

I’m Patrícia. I work across climate strategy, carbon removal, and carbon markets - bringing an operator’s lens to how climate solutions are built, financed, and scaled.

I’ve spent most of my career in climate - and the last 8+ years specifically focused on carbon removal and carbon markets - supporting both developers and buyers across the full lifecycle. This includes hands-on experience working with project developers, from integrity and MRV considerations to partnershios, portfolio strategy, contracting, pricing, and climate claims.

In 2024, I travelled extensively across the Global South, visiting projects on the ground with developers - an experience that reinforced my conviction that climate solutions require both strong execution and strong narratives that enable action at scale.

My experience spans the full ecosystem - from supplier and buyer perspectives to an investment lens, including early-stage investing as a Venture Partner at Satgana, investing in early-stage climate solutions. I am based in Paris since 2025.

I’m a native Portuguese speaker, fluent in English and Spanish, and advanced in French.
