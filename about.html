About

I’m Patricia. I work across climate strategy, carbon removal, and carbon markets - bringing an operator’s lens to how climate solutions are built, financed, and scaled.

I’ve spent most of my career in climate - and the last 8+ years specifically focused on carbon removal and carbon markets - supporting both developers and buyers across the full lifecycle. This includes hands-on experience working within project developers (e.g., 44.01), from integrity and MRV considerations to portfolio strategy, contracting, pricing, and climate claims.

In 2024, I travelled extensively across the Global South, visiting projects and working on the ground with developers - an experience that reinforced my conviction that climate solutions require both strong execution and strong narratives that enable action at scale.

Languages: Portuguese (native), English (fluent), Spanish (fluent), French (advanced).
Previously: Patch, CUR8, 44.01.

Primary education

MBA, UC Berkeley Haas School of Business (Energy & Cleantech). Civil Engineering degree and MSc in Construction Management, Instituto Superior Técnico (Lisbon).

Causes and beyond work

I care deeply about climate and social causes and have volunteered across Brazil, the US, and the UK. Outside of work, I’m a climber and hiker, and an avid traveller (64 countries to date).
