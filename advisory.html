Advisory

I advise organisations on carbon removal, voluntary carbon markets, and climate strategy - bringing an operator’s lens to how climate solutions are built, financed, and scaled.

My work combines strategic advisory and hands-on support, from analysis to execution.

It spans the ecosystem, supporting buyers, project developers, investors, and institutions - with experience on both the supplier and buyer sides, including within project developers (44.01), and previously at Patch and CUR8.

Who I advise

I also support public and philanthropic institutions working on climate strategy and market-building.

Corporates

  • Strategy for carbon removal and voluntary carbon markets
  • Procurement and portfolio construction
  • Credit quality and integrity assessment (incl. MRV and delivery risk)
  • Contracting and supplier engagement decision support
  • Narrative support for credible climate action

Project developers

  • Commercial and go-to-market strategy
  • Offtake readiness and buyer engagement
  • Investment readiness and fundraising positioning
  • MRV and integrity translated into buyer-ready positioning
  • Partnerships, commercial structuring, and market benchmarking

Investors, funds & accelerators

  • Market mapping and diligence support across pathways
  • Integrity screening and underwriting of delivery risk
  • Commercial assessment: pricing dynamics, buyer segments, scale potential
  • Founder support on go-to-market narrative and positioning

Representative examples

  • Supporting a buyer in building a carbon removal approach aligned with integrity, procurement realities, and credible claims - including supplier assessment and contracting considerations.
  • Working with a project developer to strengthen commercial readiness: positioning, partnership strategy, and what matters most to buyers in real procurement processes.
  • Advising investors on the integrity and commercial viability of carbon removal pathways, with an underwriting lens on delivery, verification and scale risk.
